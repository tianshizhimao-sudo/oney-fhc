How the score is built

Transparent scoring. No hidden levers.

Every tool rolls up to a single readiness score out of 100. Here's what goes into it, and what each band actually means.

📐

Four equal dimensions

Each specialist tool measures four dimensions worth 25 points each — liability pressure, visibility, review discipline, support — then sums to a /100 readiness score.

🎚

Bands you can act on

70+ lender-ready — focus on product choice. 45–69 workable — one or two big levers to fix. <45 real prep work — step back before applying.

🧮

Calibrated, not flattering

Weights are tuned against how real credit assessors read a file: limits > balances, ATO currency > revenue size, clean employment > promotion speed. The score rewards what banks reward.

🔍

Attention insights, not generic tips

Every result surfaces the specific items dragging your score down, in priority order. Low scores auto-expand the top items so you see them on mobile without hunting.

🛠

Investor math, not vibes

The Investor Check runs real portfolio math: usable equity at 80% LVR, 80% rent shading for serviceability, 5× gross-income borrowing proxy, and a deposit-vs-capacity check against your target.

🤝

A score isn't a decision

These tools map your position — they don't approve or decline anything. For an actual lender conversation, the broker chat is where the score becomes a plan.
