Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool really free?
Yes, completely. No credit card, no sign-up required.
Will this affect my credit score?
No. This tool does not perform any credit checks. Your data never leaves your browser.
How accurate are the results?
The score is based on the information you provide and uses general lending guidelines. It is intended as an educational guide, not a precise assessment. For an accurate picture, speak with a licensed mortgage broker.
Who built this?
This tool was built by Oney & Co — an Australian financial services company focused on helping people understand their borrowing position. Our founder has 8 years of experience in business banking.
Is my data private?
Yes. All data is processed locally in your browser and is never sent to any server. We do not collect, store or share your information.
What do the 4 dimensions measure?
Deposit (your savings relative to the property price), Income (your earning capacity), Debts (existing liabilities), and Timeline (how soon you plan to act).
I got a low score. Should I be worried?
Not at all. A lower score simply means there are areas to work on — and knowing where you stand is the first step. Many people who start with a low score go on to successfully buy a home with the right preparation.
What should I do after completing the quiz?
Review your score across each dimension and focus on the areas flagged as needing work. Consider speaking with a licensed mortgage broker when you feel ready.
