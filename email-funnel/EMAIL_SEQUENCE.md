5. Create "aha" moment that motivates action

```

How Banks Really Assess You
(It's Not What You Think)
[Chinese subtitle: 你知道银行是怎么评估你的吗？]

[SECTION 1: THE REAL ASSESSMENT RATE]

Intro Copy:
Banks don't lend at the advertised rates you see on their
websites. When assessing how much you can borrow, they apply
a stress test buffer.

[RATE BOX - Green background]

7.10%

Myth 1: "I can borrow based on my salary"
→ Reality: Banks multiply your income by 7.10%, not advertised rate.

Myth 2: "20% deposit means I'm safe"
→ Reality: Deposit is one factor. DTI and serviceability matter more.
  Even with 20%, you can be declined if expenses are too high

Myth 3: "Pre-approval means guaranteed"
→ Reality: Pre-approval is conditional. Banks re-assess everything
```

### Key Copy Points

- "7.10%" (establishes specific, authoritative rate)
- "Assessment rate" not "interest rate" (educates terminology)
- "Myth 1/2/3" structure (makes content scannable, memorable)
- "Banks re-assess everything at settlement" (key insight)
- "Using their numbers" (credibility through accuracy)

### Why Day 3?

- Day 0-2: Email 1 still processing, users in "honeymoon" phase
- Day 3: Interest is starting to fade, need re-engagement

### Open Rate Target

- Get 5-8% to click Bank-Ready Score

## Copy Principles Across All 3 Emails

### 1. **Specificity Over Generality**
- NOT: "Know more about borrowing"
- YES: "Using actual APRA buffers (7.10% assessment rate)"

### 2. **Insider Perspective**
- Dong's 8 years in banking mentioned in 2 emails
- Assessment rates, DTI, serviceability (banker terms)
- "What banks actually look at" (insider knowledge)

### 3. **Myth Busting**
- Email 2 explicitly corrects "common myths"
- Builds credibility through truth-telling
- Makes reader feel smarter

### 4. **Actionable Next Steps**
- Email 1: Learn about FHC
- Email 2: Try the Bank-Ready Score
- Email 3: Buy FHC or book call (choose your path)

### 5. **Scarcity + Urgency**
- "Early-bird price locked in" (Email 1)
- "Early-bird pricing closes at launch" (Email 3)
- Creates motivation to act

### 6. **Bilingual Awareness**
- English primary, Chinese translations for key phrases
- Chinese subject lines for Email 2 & 3 (shows respect for audience)
- Not full Chinese translation (would dilute primary English message)

## Common Questions from Recipients

**Q: Why is the assessment rate 7.10%?**
A: RBA (central bank rate) is 4.10%. Banks add 3% buffer for serviceability stress testing to ensure you can repay if rates rise.

**Q: Is this the rate I'll pay?**
A: No, this is the assessment rate for calculating borrowing power. Your actual rate depends on current market rates and your credit profile.

**Q: Why are you telling me this?**
A: Banks use this rate internally but don't advertise it. Most borrowers don't know this, so they get shocked at pre-approval. We think transparency is better.

**Q: Is the Bank-Ready Score free?**
A: Yes, the basic Bank-Ready Score is free at tools.oneyco.com.au. The full FHC toolkit is $29 early-bird ($79 regular).

**Q: What if I don't buy FHC?**
A: That's totally fine. We want you to understand how banks actually think, whether you use our tool or not.
